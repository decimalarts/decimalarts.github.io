Between nothing and something, there’s a story

Decimal Arts is an independent game studio based in Naarm/Melbourne. We create narrative-driven games rooted in place, history, and human experience — stories told through speculative futures, grounded worlds, and richly crafted characters. Our work reflects our belief that games, like all art forms, shape culture. We build carefully, ask questions deliberately, and design experiences that leave space for the audience to feel, reflect, and connect.
